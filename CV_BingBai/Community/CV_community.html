Mother Teresa Missionary, Kolkata, India


In 2012, I spent two weeks at Mother Teresa Missionaries in Kolkata, India. With other volunteers from all over the world, I served as a care giver at two of four charity houses, Dying House and Disabled Children House.

I witnessed unbearable sufferings but also omniscient compassions. It's an unforgetable experience, which opened my journey to the community services.
